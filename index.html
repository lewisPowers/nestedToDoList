THINGS TO DO:

Focus Cursor to Input Field: press '/' on keyboard

Add Item: type text into input field; press enter

Add Nested Item: hover over item you want to nest in;
click the '+' button
type text & press 'Enter'

Mark Completed: hover over item; click checkbox to the left

Edit Item: hover over item; hold the shift key and click the text

Delete Item: hover over item; click the 'X'

Move Item: click and drag to desired priority in list
if dragging up, release dragged element directly over the item

Nesting Items: option 1) click item's text to nest it into the item above it.
option 2) click and drag item to the right.

Unnesting Item:option 1) if fully nested, click item to unnest one layer.
option 2) click and drag item slightly to the left.

Unnesting Item Completely: click and drag item greatly to the left.
